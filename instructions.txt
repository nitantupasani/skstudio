HAALAAT VIDEO LINK (Preview Box)
Haalaat Stills
Role
- Director, Cinematographer, Editor, Poetry, Producer
Synopsis
-
A film made in COVID-19 lockdown at my home. Caught up in
stillness. Thoughts like void in an empty box. No sense of the
passing time. Emptiness. Anxieties, Ecstasies and mixed hopes for
an uncertain tomorrow.

Home Page ——>About
Hello! I am filmmaker based out of Nagpur with 10 years of experience in filmmaking. I like
engaging in films, as a medium to empathise and connect with people and places. I see it as a
way of storytelling, rooted in observation and reflection. Films help me build a worldview and give
a voice to express. My films have stemmed from either a personal emotion or a quest about the
surroundings. Some of my films have been screened at various international film festivals like
IDSFFK Kerala, MIFF Mumbai, IFFSA Toronto, Cinemasala Film Festival, Switzerland; and won
awards at CHINH Youth Film Festival, Delhi and Quarantine International Film Festival.
2019:
- Bachelor’s in Mass Media at St. Xavier’s College, Mumbai
- Film Appreciation Course, FTTI, Pune
2021-22:
- Attended multiple filmmaking workshops and did on-field documentary productions in Auroville,
Ladakh and Rann of Kutch. Also attended Writing, Acting and Film Analysis workshops at
Auroville Film Institute.
-
- - Curated and Organised 25+ Film screenings and Q&A with filmmakers at Meraki Theatre,
Nagpur.
- Participated in Let’s Doc, Kolkata as an observer and in PSBT Doccommune with a work-in-
progress film.
2023-24:
- Worked with documentary filmmaker Pankaj Rishi Kumar on multiple schedules a
cinematographer and location sound recordist in Nagpur, Digras, Khammam
- As a fellow at Auroville Film Institute, I worked on Audio-Visual Archiving, Conducted
Daily study circles and organise workshops and events.
2024-25:
- Managed Social Media and Outreach for Nagpur Edition of Pune Int. Film Festival
2026:
- Pursuing Diploma in Indian Aesthetics at Jnanpravaha, Mumbai
Working on a documentary, while wondering, writing.
